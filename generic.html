About me

Hi, I am an immersive designer with a lot of love for illustrations.I have a background in UI and UX design as well as making large and small scale illustrations

I am currently in my last year of interaction design,I love making illustrations and have been working on them for the majority of the past two years.I have been taking and working with a lot of large and small scale institutions such as recreational/golf clubs or just individual people who want illustrations for their home or work place.I have been taking an interest in immersive design and have been learning the basic tools for it, I am beginner in softwares such as blender and unity.I plan on learning how to code for unity as well as three.js in the near future.Acquiring these skills will let me pursue immersive design for my further education where I plan on working on and building a new future for technology and design.I hope this will lead me to design for immersive training and sports where I will be ultimately be able to work with football clubs.
